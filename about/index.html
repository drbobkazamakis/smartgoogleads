Most published “PPC tools” lists pretend Google Ads is just one of several channels. It isn’t — Google has its own gravitational pull, its own quirks (Performance Max, Demand Gen, Smart Bidding), its own native tooling. Tools either work with that gravity or fight against it. This site reviews tools specifically for advertisers running Google Ads as their primary channel.

Why Google Ads–specific reviews

The interaction with Google’s native bidding and campaign types matters more than most reviewers acknowledge. A tool that’s great on Meta but fights Smart Bidding inside Google Ads earns a different review than one built around Google’s ecosystem. The tool list reflects that distinction.

What I won’t do

Sponsored placements, affiliate arrangements — declined.
